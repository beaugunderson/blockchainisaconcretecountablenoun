Concrete?

A noun can be concrete or abstract. Concrete nouns represent things that can be interacted with individually.

  • You can interact with a blockchain
  • You can interact with blockchains
  • You can not interact with blockchain

A comparable concrete noun is “cell phone”. Replace “blockchain” with “cell phone” in a sentence. If it sounds wrong you know you've messed up.:

  • IBM is investing in blockchain technology
  • IBM is investing in cell phone technology
  • IBM is investing in blockchain
  • IBM is investing in cell phone
  • Blockchains are getting lots of news coverage now
  • Cell phones are getting lots of news coverage now
  • Blockchain is getting lots of news coverage now
  • Cell phone is getting lots of news coverage new

Countable?

A noun can be countable or uncountable.
